Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Safety First! Always obey the rules of the road. Obey all traffic signals, signs, and laws. Get in the mindset of “driving” your bike—not just “riding” your bike. This will help you be a more focused and legally compliant bike rider.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

Check helmet for visible signs of wear like cracks, dents, or frayed straps. Also, if its been involved in a crash or is over five years old, its time for a replacement, as helmets can lose their protective qualities over time

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Before riding test the brakes to ensure they are functioning well, and make sure the chain and gears are clean and lubricated. If everything looks good, it should be safe to ride.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

Obey Signals, Wear Helmet, Stay Focused, Watch for Cars etc. and many more.
